Before you pick files

  • Nothing is uploaded. Your sessions are read and analyzed entirely inside this browser tab.
  • No network requests. This page talks to no server — you can disconnect from the internet and it still works.
  • Stored only on this device. Parsed sessions live in your browser's local IndexedDB so dashboards survive a refresh. They never leave this machine. "Clear my saved data" in the footer wipes them.
Your transcripts can contain proprietary code and private conversations. That's exactly why this runs locally — the data has nowhere else to go.
